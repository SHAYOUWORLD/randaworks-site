Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

This English page is provided for convenience. The Japanese version is the authoritative source if there is any inconsistency. For title-specific handling, see the Inga Chronicle privacy policy.

RandaWorks ("we", "our", or "us") sets out below how we handle user information on the website https://www.randaworks.com (the "Site").

1. Information we collect

We may collect personal information such as your name and email address when you contact us through our forms or email.

2. How we use information

We use collected information for the following purposes.

  • To reply to inquiries
  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Site and related services

3. Analytics

We use analytics tools to understand how the Site is used and to improve it.

(1) Google Analytics 4

The Site may use Google Analytics 4 provided by Google LLC. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect information such as pages viewed, referrer information, browser and device information, and session duration.

For more details, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.

(2) Microsoft Clarity

The Site uses Microsoft Clarity, provided by Microsoft Corporation. Clarity collects behavioral analytics such as heatmaps, session recordings, clicks, scrolls, and mouse movements to help us understand how visitors interact with the Site. This data is sent to and stored on Microsoft's servers.

For details on how Microsoft handles data, see Microsoft Privacy Statement.

(3) Internal analytics

We also operate our own analytics setup to understand page views, clicks, referrers, browser and device information, and other usage signals. We do not use this setup to directly identify individuals.

4. Cookies and similar technologies

The Site may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics, behavioral analysis (Microsoft Clarity), FormSubmit.co contact form handling, CAPTCHA verification, spam protection, and advertising measurement.

You can restrict or disable cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the Site may not work as expected.

5. Advertising

We may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdSense to support site operations.

Those services may use cookies to show personalized or non-personalized ads and to measure performance. You can manage ad settings through Google's controls or industry opt-out tools.

6. Third-party services

We may rely on third-party providers for hosting, analytics, advertising, email delivery, and contact form processing. Our contact form uses FormSubmit.co, which may handle your name, email address, message content, and CAPTCHA verification data to process submissions, deliver notification emails, and reduce spam.

7. Sharing with third parties

We do not provide personal information to third parties except where required by law, where you consent, or where handling is delegated to service providers for site operations.

8. Retention

We retain information only for as long as needed for the purposes described above, then delete it or otherwise handle it appropriately.

9. Your rights

If you want to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information we hold, please contact us. We will respond within a reasonable scope in accordance with applicable law.

10. Revisions

We may update this policy when needed. Any updates will be posted on this page.

11. Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact us below.

Email: contact@randaworks.com
