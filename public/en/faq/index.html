FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about RandaWorks and Inga Chronicle.

Where can I play Inga Chronicle?

You can try the PC browser demo on the game page. You can also add it to your wishlist on the Steam store page.

Game page View on Steam

What languages are supported?

The game is primarily in Japanese. The website has a growing number of English pages. Broader language support is planned for future updates.

Will it come to other platforms?

The confirmed platform is Steam (Windows). Other platforms are not yet decided. Announcements will be made on the site and Steam when plans are finalized.

How do I report a bug?

Use the game support page or the contact form. Including steps to reproduce, your OS, and browser version helps us investigate faster.

What is RandaWorks?

An indie game studio building games where understanding and discovery become the fun. Learn more on the About page.
