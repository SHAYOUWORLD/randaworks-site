Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

This page is the title-specific privacy policy for Inga Chronicle. For cookies, advertising, and analytics on the website as a whole, please see the site-wide privacy policy. This English page is provided for convenience; the Japanese version is the authoritative source if there is any inconsistency.

RandaWorks ("we", "our", or "us") sets out below how we handle user information for the game Inga Chronicle (the "App").

1. Scope

This policy applies to information we obtain and use in connection with the App.

For platform functions provided by Steam (including Steamworks), information collected and handled by Valve is governed by Valve's Privacy Policy.

2. Information we may obtain

We may obtain the following information to provide and improve the App.

(1) Information for operation and quality improvement

  • Crash logs or error logs, such as device or OS information, error timing, and error details
  • Usage information, such as play counts, progress, settings, and aggregate play patterns

We do not require players to enter directly identifying information such as name, address, or phone number within the App itself.

(2) Information related to Steamworks features

We may use Steamworks APIs to provide platform features such as achievements. In that process, identifiers linked to a Steam account, achievement status, or other information necessary to provide those features may be referenced or transmitted through Steamworks.

3. Purposes of use

We use the information above for the following purposes.

  • To provide, maintain, and operate the App
  • To investigate bugs, improve quality, and improve features
  • To provide Steamworks features such as achievements
  • To handle inquiries, communication, and identity confirmation where necessary
  • To prevent misuse, exploitation of bugs, and security incidents

4. Legal basis for EEA/UK users

When processing information relating to users in the EEA or UK, we rely on one or more of the following bases: legitimate interests in operating and improving the App, performance of a contract, or compliance with legal obligations.

5. Third parties and processors

We do not provide user information to third parties except where required by law. We may rely on service providers to process information where necessary for operation of the App.

  • Steam / Steamworks (Valve Corporation) for platform features such as achievements
  • Providers of crash analytics, log storage, or similar support services, where introduced

6. International transfers

Where external services such as Steam / Steamworks are operated outside Japan, information may be processed or stored outside Japan.

7. Cookies and similar technologies

The App itself does not use cookies. The official website may rely on external services such as FormSubmit.co for session handling or spam prevention, and the Steam platform may use cookies according to Valve's own policies.

8. Retention

We retain information only for as long as needed for the purposes above, then delete, anonymize, or otherwise handle it appropriately. Information may be retained for a reasonable period where needed for compliance, fraud prevention, or security.

9. Security measures

We take reasonable measures to protect user information, including access control, limiting storage locations, and protecting communications where appropriate.

10. Inquiry information

When you contact us, we may receive the following information.

  • Name (optional)
  • Email address
  • Message content

We use this information to reply and handle support requests, and we retain it only as long as reasonably necessary.

11. Your rights

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of information we hold, subject to verification and applicable law. For information controlled by Valve through Steamworks, we may need to direct you to Valve's own procedures.

12. Minors

If you are a minor, please use the App only with the consent of a parent or legal guardian.

13. Revisions

We may revise this policy in response to legal changes, new features, or operational changes. Revisions will be posted on this page and, where appropriate, announced in the App or on the distribution page.

14. Governing law and jurisdiction

This policy is governed by the laws of Japan. The Tokyo District Court has exclusive jurisdiction as the court of first instance for disputes concerning this policy.

15. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us below.
