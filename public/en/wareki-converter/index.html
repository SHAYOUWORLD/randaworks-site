How to use

  1. Use the left panel to convert from Western year. Use the right panel to convert from Japanese era.
  2. Enter a year and click "Convert" to see the result immediately.
  3. Change the value and click again to convert multiple years in sequence.

FAQ

What can this Japanese era converter do?

Convert between Western years and Japanese era years (Meiji, Taishō, Shōwa, Heisei, Reiwa) in both directions.

Does it handle year 1 (gannen)?

Yes. Year 1 (gannen, 元年) is treated as year 1. For example, Reiwa 1 = 2019, Heisei 1 = 1989.

What time periods are supported?

This tool supports Meiji era (1868) onwards. Older eras and lunar calendar conversions are not included.

Can I use it for official documents?

This is suitable for quick reference only. For important paperwork such as legal applications, please verify with official sources.
