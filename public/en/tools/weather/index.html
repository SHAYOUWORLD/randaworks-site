Past Weather Lookup

1940 — Present

Select a date and city to look up historical weather data. Perfect for revisiting the weather on a birthday or any special day.

Select a date and city, then click Search.

What you can do

Check historical temperaturesCompare max and min temperatures to see how hot or cold a particular day was.
Precipitation and windSee rainfall and wind speed, not just sunny or rainy.
Birthdays and eventsPerfect for checking weather on memorable dates like birthdays or historical events.
Major city presetsQuick access to Tokyo, Osaka, and other major Japanese cities.

How to use

  1. Pick a date and select a city from the dropdown.
  2. Click "Search" to see the weather data in card format.
  3. Try a preset first, then explore any date you like.

FAQ

What does this tool show?

Historical daily weather for a specified date and city: maximum temperature, minimum temperature, precipitation, and maximum wind speed.

How far back can I search?

Data is available from 1940 onwards. Very recent dates may not be available due to archive API delays.

What cities are available?

Major Japanese cities via dropdown: Tokyo, Osaka, Nagoya, Fukuoka, Sapporo, Sendai, Hiroshima, Kyoto, Niigata, and Naha.

Why no data for some dates?

The external archive API may have missing data for certain dates, or the date may be out of range. Try a different date.
