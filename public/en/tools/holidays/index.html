FAQ

What can the holiday checker do?

Select a year and country to see public holidays and long weekends (3+ consecutive days). Useful for travel planning, business days, and content research.

Which countries are supported?

Japan, US, UK, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Canada, Australia, and South Korea. Holiday systems differ by country, so results vary.

How are long weekends calculated?

We detect consecutive periods of 3 or more days combining holidays and weekends. Does not account for additional paid time off.

What if no results appear?

The year may be out of range or the external API may be temporarily unavailable. Try a different year or try again later.
